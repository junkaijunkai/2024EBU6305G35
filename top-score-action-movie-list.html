Top Score Action Movie List

Explore the best action movies.

1.
The Dark Knight Rises (2012)

Eight years after the death of District Attorney Harvey Dent, Gotham City has enjoyed a period of relative peace under the leadership of Commissioner Gordon. However, the emergence of the terrorist leader Bane brings Gotham to the brink of destruction. Bruce Wayne, who has been missing for years, must once again don the cape and cowl of Batman to save the city that has branded him an enemy.

2.
The Bourne Ultimatum (2007)

Jason Bourne continues his quest to find out who he is. In this installment of the Bourne series, Bourne travels to New York City, Moscow, Paris, and London to uncover the truth behind his identity and the conspiracy that surrounds him.

3.
Avengers: Endgame (2019)

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War, the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to undo Thanos' actions and restore order to the universe.

4.
Mad Max: Fury Road (2015)

In a post-apocalyptic wasteland, Max teams up with Imperator Furiosa to escape from a tyrannical warlord who controls the water supply. As they flee across the desert in a war rig, they encounter allies and enemies alike in a desperate bid for freedom.

5.
The Bourne Identity (2002)

A man is found floating in the Mediterranean Sea with no memory of his past. As he pieces together his identity, he discovers that he is skilled in combat and espionage. Pursued by unknown assailants, he must uncover the truth about his past while staying one step ahead of his enemies.

6.
The Bourne Supremacy (2004)

Jason Bourne is framed for a botched CIA operation and must clear his name while evading capture. As he digs deeper into the conspiracy, he uncovers shocking truths about his past and the government's involvement in illegal activities.

7.
Face/Off (1997)

An FBI agent and a terrorist switch faces in a groundbreaking medical procedure. As they assume each other's identities, they engage in a deadly game of cat and mouse, each determined to destroy the other's life.

8.
The Rock (1996)

A group of rogue Marines take over Alcatraz Island and threaten to launch missiles at San Francisco. An FBI chemist and a former SAS captain are sent in to stop them, leading to a high-stakes battle for control of the island.

9.
Gladiator (2000)

After being betrayed and left for dead, a Roman general seeks revenge against the corrupt emperor who murdered his family and sent him into slavery. He rises through the ranks of the gladiatorial arena to challenge the emperor and restore honor to Rome.

10.
Mission: Impossible - Ghost Protocol (2011)

IMF agent Ethan Hunt and his team are blamed for a terrorist attack on the Kremlin and must go rogue to clear their names. With the help of a mysterious assassin, they uncover a plot to ignite a global nuclear war and must race against time to prevent disaster.
