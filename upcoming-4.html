Strangers When We Meet

2024 | Drama, Crime | 1h48min

Plot

The film is adapted from the true story "The Murderous Girl in a Wedding Dress". Lao Qin (played by Fan Wei), a released prisoner, was released from prison after serving 27 years. He just wanted to get married and have children and start a new life, but unexpectedly met Chang Juan (played by Zhou Dongyu), a girl who had also just been released from prison. He asked for a child, she asked for wealth, and the two of them, who were both "reduced from the world", began a profit marriage with their own purposes.
