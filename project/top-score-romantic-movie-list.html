Titanic (1997)

On April 10, 1912, the luxurious passenger liner Titanic embarked on its maiden voyage from Southampton, England to New York, USA. Rich girl Rose (Kate Winslet) and her mother, along with her fiancé Cal, boarded the first-class cabin; on the other side, rebellious young artist Jack (Leonardo DiCaprio) won a third-class ticket in a gamble at the dock. Rose, tired of the hypocrisy of upper-class life and unwilling to marry Cal, decides to commit suicide by jumping into the sea but is saved by Jack. Soon, the beautiful and lively Rose falls in love with the handsome and cheerful Jack. As their relationship deepens, Jack takes Rose to a third-class dance and paints her portrait, and their feelings for each other gradually intensify. On the calm and quiet night of April 14, 1912, Titanic struck an iceberg. Faced with the fate of sinking, the "unsinkable" Titanic faced its demise, and the budding love between Rose and Jack would also undergo the test of life and death.
